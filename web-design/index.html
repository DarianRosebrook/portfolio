The Meeting

This is a meeting to get to know what it is you and your brand stand for. Because of the volume of design requests, I choose carefully who I partner with. This helps ensure that your brand's needs and goals are at the forefront of the designed solution.

The Discovery

We'll need to assess the existing content In order for anything to be designed effectively, the message must be defined. From here we can start to build the proposal.

The R & D

Once the first payment is received we can then get started on the work. It takes a thorough process of research and development to get to the final concept. Based on the points throughout this section, the final result will be a high quality solution tailored for the project goals.

Deployment

Once the project is complete and ready for deployment, I will take you through the case-study and describe the design decisions made along the way to arrive at the most effective version of the final product. After the final payment, deliverables will be sent.
